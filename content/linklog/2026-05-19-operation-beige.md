I learned about the *Operation B.E.I.G.E* theory in Gabrielle Minoli's instagram account (`@saltlacroix`), and... I just had to know more about this concept. What a visionary woman.

>In her own words, B.E.I.G.E refers to media, trends and shoppable items that are “‘Boring and Engineered to Identify with Gentrification and Eugenics.’” While she admits she initially thought the “Eugenics” part of the acronym might be a stretch, “clearly some big brands had other plans with their campaigns this year”.
